MongoDB Atlas and Voyage AI are now native plugins in Dify's marketplace, letting developers and no-code builders visually compose AI workflows and agents that connect directly to MongoDB data and perform semantic retrieval with Atlas Vector Search alongside Voyage AI embeddings and reranking. The integration ships a complete RAG pipeline template that accepts user input, embeds queries with Voyage AI, searches MongoDB Atlas, reranks results, and formats output through a single visual canvas.
