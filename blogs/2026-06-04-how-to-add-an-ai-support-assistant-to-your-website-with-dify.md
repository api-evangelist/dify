This tutorial guides users through building and embedding an AI chatbot on their website using Dify's no-code platform. The process involves selecting a template, uploading company knowledge, refining the system prompt, testing responses, and embedding a snippet into your site. The assistant answers customer questions directly from your content, reducing visitor drop-off without requiring redesign or engineering resources.
